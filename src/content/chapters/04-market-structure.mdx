<p class="ch-intro">The single most important skill in trading. Before any entry, you must read the trend. Structure tells you the story of what price is doing.</p>
<div class="g2">
  <div class="card glass-green"><div class="badge-pill bp-green">Bullish Trend</div><h3>Uptrend = HH + HL</h3><p>Higher Highs: each peak is higher than the last. Higher Lows: each pullback is higher than the last. Buyers in control. Only take BUY trades.</p></div>
  <div class="card glass-red"><div class="badge-pill bp-red">Bearish Trend</div><h3>Downtrend = LH + LL</h3><p>Lower Highs: each peak is lower than the last. Lower Lows: each bottom is lower. Sellers in control. Only take SELL trades.</p></div>
</div>
<div class="card glass-gold" style="margin-top:24px">
  <div class="badge-pill bp-gold">Key Concepts</div>
  <h3>BOS and CHoCH</h3>
  <p style="margin-bottom:12px"><strong style="color:#fff">Break of Structure (BOS)</strong> — Price breaks a previous significant high (bullish BOS) or low (bearish BOS), confirming the trend continues. Trade in this direction.</p>
  <p><strong style="color:#fff">Change of Character (CHoCH)</strong> — Price breaks structure in the OPPOSITE direction for the first time. Early signal that trend may be reversing. Wait for confirmation.</p>
</div>
