<div class="accord"><div class="accord-head"><span class="atitle">🟢 Bullish Engulfing — Strong Buy Signal</span><span class="aicon">+</span></div><div class="accord-body"><p>Large green candle completely engulfs the previous red candle's body. Buyers have overwhelmed sellers. Use at support zones, end of downtrend, at retest levels after a break.</p></div></div>
<div class="accord"><div class="accord-head"><span class="atitle">🔴 Bearish Engulfing — Strong Sell Signal</span><span class="aicon">+</span></div><div class="accord-body"><p>Large red candle swallows the previous green candle. Sellers have overwhelmed buyers. Use at resistance zones, end of uptrend.</p></div></div>
<div class="accord"><div class="accord-head"><span class="atitle">📍 Pin Bar / Hammer — Rejection Candle</span><span class="aicon">+</span></div><div class="accord-body"><p>Long wick with small body. Wick shows price tried to go in a direction but was forcefully rejected. Wick must be at least 2× the size of the body.</p></div></div>
<div class="accord"><div class="accord-head"><span class="atitle">⭐ Morning Star / Evening Star — 3-Candle Reversal</span><span class="aicon">+</span></div><div class="accord-body"><p><strong style="color:#fff">Morning Star (Bullish):</strong> Large bearish → small doji → large bullish. At bottom of downtrend at support. <strong style="color:#fff">Evening Star (Bearish):</strong> Large bullish → doji → large bearish. Top of uptrend at resistance.</p></div></div>
<div class="highlight-box">
  <h4>The Golden Rule</h4>
  <p>A candlestick pattern by itself is just a signal. It only becomes a trade when it appears at a valid AOI, in the direction of the trend, after a break and retest. Stack 3+ confluences — then enter.</p>
</div>
