<p class="ch-intro">Knowledge without action is nothing. Here is the exact path from where you are now to consistent profitability.</p>
<div class="tl">
  <div class="tl-item"><h4>Month 1–2: Foundation</h4><p>Master market structure, candlestick reading, AOI identification. Paper trade only — no real money. Study 1 chart for 2 hours daily. Journal everything.</p></div>
  <div class="tl-item"><h4>Month 3–4: Strategy Drilling</h4><p>Backtest break-and-retest strategy on 3 months of historical data. Only open a live account when 60%+ win rate on paper with 1:2 R:R. Open micro account with max $100.</p></div>
  <div class="tl-item"><h4>Month 5–6: SMC Integration</h4><p>Layer order blocks and FVGs into your existing framework. Study 1 institutional setup per day. Watch how price sweeps liquidity before moving.</p></div>
  <div class="tl-item"><h4>Month 7–9: Live Trading</h4><p>Micro account. Max $20 risk per trade. Focus on executing your system perfectly, not on profit. Keep a detailed trade journal. Review every Sunday.</p></div>
  <div class="tl-item"><h4>Month 10–12: Scaling</h4><p>3 consecutive profitable months on micro → scale to mini account. Keep same risk %. System doesn't change — position size does.</p></div>
  <div class="tl-item"><h4>Year 2–3: Professional</h4><p>You understand your edge deeply. Seen hundreds of setups. Losers don't shake you. Winners don't excite you. This emotional neutrality is when trading becomes a real business.</p></div>
</div>
<div class="highlight-box" style="margin-top:40px"><h4>The One Truth</h4><p>Trading is a marathon. The traders who make consistent money are not the smartest — they are the most disciplined and most patient. Every professional trader lost money for years before becoming consistently profitable. Losing is not failure. Quitting is.</p></div>
