  <div class="card"><div class="badge-pill bp-gold">Majors</div><h3>Major Pairs</h3><p>Always include USD. Highest liquidity, lowest spreads. EUR/USD, GBP/USD, USD/JPY, AUD/USD. Best pairs to start.</p></div>
  <div class="card"><div class="badge-pill bp-blue">Minors</div><h3>Minor / Cross Pairs</h3><p>No USD. EUR/GBP, EUR/JPY, GBP/JPY. Higher spreads, good for diversity once experienced.</p></div>
  <div class="card"><div class="badge-pill bp-red">Exotics</div><h3>Exotic Pairs</h3><p>One major + one emerging market currency. USD/INR, USD/TRY. Very high spreads. Avoid as a beginner.</p></div>
</div>
<div class="highlight-box">
  <h4>The Sweet Spot</h4>
  <p>The most successful independent traders use hybrid day + swing trading. Identify setups on higher timeframes (4H/Daily), execute on lower timeframes (15m/1H). Fewer, higher-quality trades always beat overtrading.</p>
