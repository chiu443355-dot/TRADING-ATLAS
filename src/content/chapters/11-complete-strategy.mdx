<p class="ch-intro">Everything you've learned, distilled into a repeatable step-by-step entry system. This is your playbook.</p>
<div class="card glass-gold">
  <div class="badge-pill bp-gold">The Confluence Framework</div>
  <h3>5-Step Entry Checklist</h3>
  <div class="tl">
    <div class="tl-item"><h4>Step 1 — Higher Timeframe Trend</h4><p>Check Daily and 4H. Clear HH+HL or LH+LL structure? Only trade IN the direction of the higher timeframe trend.</p></div>
    <div class="tl-item"><h4>Step 2 — Identify the AOI</h4><p>Find the nearest valid area of interest — 3+ touches, 5–60 pips wide. Is price approaching this zone right now?</p></div>
    <div class="tl-item"><h4>Step 3 — Break and Retest</h4><p>Has the key level been cleanly broken with a body close? Is price retesting it? This confirms the zone has "flipped."</p></div>
    <div class="tl-item"><h4>Step 4 — Entry Candlestick Signal</h4><p>At the zone on 15m or 1H, look for a bullish engulfing, pin bar, or morning star. No pattern = no entry.</p></div>
    <div class="tl-item"><h4>Step 5 — Risk Management</h4><p>SL: below the zone with 5-pip buffer. TP: next key resistance or minimum 1:2 R:R. Position size: 1–2% account risk.</p></div>
  </div>
</div>
<div class="accord" style="margin-top:24px"><div class="accord-head"><span class="atitle">Strategy 1 — Never Trade Inside a Range</span><span class="aicon">+</span></div><div class="accord-body"><p>When price is between clear support and resistance, do NOT trade. Wait for liquidity to be swept. Entry after the sweep, not during the range.</p></div></div>
<div class="accord"><div class="accord-head"><span class="atitle">Strategy 2 — Second Retest Entry</span><span class="aicon">+</span></div><div class="accord-body"><p>After a confirmed breakout and retest, wait for the SECOND retest of the key zone. The second, if the zone holds, has very high probability.</p></div></div>
<div class="accord"><div class="accord-head"><span class="atitle">Strategy 3 — FVG + Order Block Combo</span><span class="aicon">+</span></div><div class="accord-body"><p>When a Fair Value Gap aligns with an Order Block at the same price level, the confluence is extremely powerful. One of the highest-probability setups in SMC trading.</p></div></div>
<div class="accord"><div class="accord-head"><span class="atitle">Strategy 4 — Liquidity Sweep Reversal</span><span class="aicon">+</span></div><div class="accord-body"><p>Identify key support with obvious stop losses below it. Wait for price to sweep below then immediately reverse back above. Enter on the candle that closes back above. You enter exactly where smart money enters.</p></div></div>
