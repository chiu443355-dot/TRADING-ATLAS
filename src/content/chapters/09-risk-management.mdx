<p class="ch-intro">This is not optional. This is why 95% lose. You can have the best strategy in the world and blow your account without this.</p>
<div class="warn-box"><h4>Reality Check</h4><p>Even the best traders lose 40–60% of their trades. Profitability comes not from winning every trade, but from ensuring your wins are bigger than your losses. Risk management IS the edge.</p></div>
<div class="rule"><div class="rnum">01</div><div><h4>Always Use a Stop Loss</h4><p>No trade should ever be open without a stop loss. A trade without a stop is a trade without a plan. One bad trade without a stop can wipe your entire account.</p></div></div>
<div class="rule"><div class="rnum">02</div><div><h4>Minimum 1:2 Risk-to-Reward</h4><p>For every $1 you risk, target at least $2. At 1:2, you can lose 6 out of 10 trades and still be profitable.</p></div></div>
<div class="rule"><div class="rnum">03</div><div><h4>Risk Only 1–2% Per Trade</h4><p>On a $1,000 account, risk max $20 per trade. This means 20 consecutive losses to lose 40%. Aggressive risking feels exciting until you blow up in 5 trades.</p></div></div>
<div class="rule"><div class="rnum">04</div><div><h4>Set It and Forget It</h4><p>Once entered, leave it. Don't move your stop when it's about to be hit. Don't close early because you're nervous. Trust your analysis or don't enter.</p></div></div>
<div class="rule"><div class="rnum">05</div><div><h4>No Trading During High-Impact News</h4><p>NFP, interest rate decisions, CPI can move price 100+ pips in seconds. Check Forex Factory. Avoid 30 minutes before and after red-folder events.</p></div></div>
<div class="rule"><div class="rnum">06</div><div><h4>Trailing Stop Loss for Big Wins</h4><p>Once a trade moves 1R in profit, move stop to breakeven. Trail your stop to lock in profits. You'll never lose on a trade that was once winning by 1R+.</p></div></div>
<div class="highlight-box" style="margin-top:28px"><h4>The Math Is Simple</h4><p>50% win rate + 1:2 R:R = profitable system. 40% win rate + 1:3 R:R = very profitable. You don't need to win most trades. You need your wins to dwarf your losses.</p></div>
