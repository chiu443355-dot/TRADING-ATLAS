<p class="ch-intro">Before charts, candles, or strategies — understand what trading is. Not gambling. Not luck. A business of probabilities.</p>

<div class="card glass-gold">
  <div class="badge-pill bp-gold">Definition</div>
  <h3>Trading in Plain English</h3>
  <p>Buying an asset at one price and selling at a higher price — or selling first and buying back cheaper ("shorting"). You profit on the difference. In forex, you're betting one currency will strengthen or weaken relative to another.</p>
</div>

<div class="g2">
  <div class="card glass-green">
    <div class="badge-pill bp-green">Long Trade</div>
    <h3>Going Long (Buy)</h3>
    <p>You believe price goes UP. Buy first, sell later. Example: Buy EUR/USD at 1.0800, sell at 1.0900 = 100 pips profit.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="card glass-red">
    <div class="badge-pill bp-red">Short Trade</div>
    <h3>Going Short (Sell)</h3>
    <p>You believe price goes DOWN. Sell first, buy back cheaper. Example: Sell GBP/USD at 1.2700, buy at 1.2600 = 100 pips profit.</p>
  </div>
</div>

<div class="highlight-box">
  <h4>The Core Truth</h4>
  <p>95% of retail traders lose — not because trading is impossible, but because they trade without a system. The market isn't random. Institutions control it. Understanding their behaviour is the edge.</p>
</div>

<div class="g3" style="margin-top:32px">
  <div class="card"><div class="badge-pill bp-gold">PIP</div><h3>What is a Pip?</h3><p>Smallest standard price movement. For EUR/USD: 1 pip = 0.0001. Price 1.0800 → 1.0900 = 100 pips.</p></div>
  <div class="card"><div class="badge-pill bp-blue">LOT</div><h3>Lot Sizes</h3><p>Standard = 100,000 units. Mini = 10,000. Micro = 1,000. Beginners use micro lots.</p></div>
  <div class="card"><div class="badge-pill bp-purple">LEVERAGE</div><h3>Leverage</h3><p>Control large positions with small capital. 1:100 leverage = control $10,000 with $100.</p></div>
  <div class="card"><div class="badge-pill bp-red">SPREAD</div><h3>Spread</h3><p>Gap between buy (ask) and sell (bid) price. EUR/USD has lowest spreads — best pair to start.</p></div>
  <div class="card"><div class="badge-pill bp-green">MARGIN</div><h3>Margin</h3><p>Deposit your broker holds to keep a leveraged trade open. Losses eat into margin → margin call.</p></div>
  <div class="card"><div class="badge-pill bp-gold">SWAP</div><h3>Overnight Swap</h3><p>Interest paid/received for holding a trade overnight. Swing traders must account for this.</p></div>
</div>
