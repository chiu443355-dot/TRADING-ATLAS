<div class="highlight-box">
  <h4>What Is a Prop Firm?</h4>
  <p>A proprietary trading firm provides you with capital to trade in exchange for a share of your profits (typically 70–90% goes to you). You take a paid "challenge" to prove your skills, pass it, and receive a funded account. Your personal financial risk is limited to the challenge fee — usually $100–$600.</p>
</div>

<div class="warn-box">
  <h4>Why Most Traders Fail Challenges</h4>
  <p>Not because of their strategy — because of psychology under pressure. Knowing a ₹20,000 challenge fee is at stake changes how you trade. You overtrade to hit targets faster. You move stop losses to avoid hitting the daily loss limit. You break every rule you've learned. The solution: trade the challenge exactly as you trade your demo. If you can't, you're not ready yet.</p>
</div>
